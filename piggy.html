Piggy is an episodic horror game that incorporates elements from Peppa Pig and the indie horror game Granny into a infection setting. The games' style of episodic storytelling resulted in a significant fanbase developing prior to the game's finale on May 25, 2020. Piggy was uploaded to the site in January 2020, and had been played nearly 5 billion times as of July 2020. A sequel, titled Piggy: Book 2, released on September 12, 2020.
