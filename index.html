Welcome to Roblox


Roblox is a global platform that brings people together through play that has been around since 2006. David Baszucki created Roblox and his username is builderman. Anyone can create a game with the app roblox studio. In roblox, you can customise your own avatar. Some avatar items are free, some cost robux which is the currency of roblox, while some needs to be earnt.

There are many events in roblox. RB Battles and the Metaverse Spring Hunt are one of them! In RB Battles, famous roblox youtubers battle in different games and reach the finale! The winner of the finale will earn 1 millon robux! In the Metaverse Spring Hunt, chest will be hidden around 180 different games, waiting to be found by players. The event is spread into four weeks, with up to 48 games a week and 10-12 for each champion. Players are able to participate in all of the champion's quests and activities if chosen.
